Form — From standing, step one foot back and lower straight down until both knees hit 90°. The back knee hovers just above the floor. Push through the front heel to stand, then switch legs.
Balance — Left fingertips on a wall are fine for stability. Right arm stays relaxed at your side.

Dead Bugs
3 sets × 10 per side
✓
Opposite arm + leg extend · lower back stays flat
0 / 20
Set 1 of 3 · 20 = 10/side
Form — On your back, knees and hips at 90° (tabletop). Slowly extend one leg and the opposite arm until they hover above the floor, then return. Your lower back must stay pressed into the mattress the whole time.
Arm note — Only the left arm extends. The right arm rests bent on your chest — the legs still alternate normally.

Pallof Holds
3 sets × 20–30 sec · optional
✓
Band pulls sideways · you don't move · that's the exercise
0s
Set 1 of 3
Form — Anchor a band at chest height to your side. Hold it with both hands at your chest, arms out. The band tries to twist you toward the anchor — your job is to not let it. Face each side per set.
With a broken arm — Only if completely pain-free in the right hand. No band, or any discomfort? Skip it — this one is optional right now.
