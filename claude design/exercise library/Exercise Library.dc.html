Glute Bridge
Drive through your heels — squeeze glutes at the top
Glutes Hamstrings
Beginner
Bodyweight
Form
1Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat and hip-width apart.
2Press through your heels and lift your hips until knees, hips, and shoulders form a straight line.
3Pause and squeeze the glutes, then lower with control.
Common mistakes
•Overarching the lower back — brace your core instead of hinging from the spine.
•Pushing through the toes — keep weight in the heels to load the glutes.
•Rushing the rep — hold the top for a beat before lowering.
Adaptations
Limited knee flexion — reduce range of motion, stop before discomfort.
